Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

Powerfull Solar (“Powerfull Solar,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you visit our website or submit information through our forms.

Information We Collect

When you voluntarily submit information through our website, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Mobile phone number
  • Information related to your solar or energy inquiry

How We Use Your Information

Information is used to respond to your inquiry and to send related follow-ups and reminders after you opt in. This may include solar consultations, energy audits, scheduling, and related communications you request.

SMS Communications & Consent

Powerfull Solar sends SMS messages only after a user has explicitly opted in via an optional checkbox (not pre-selected) on our website forms.

By opting in, you consent to receive recurring SMS messages related to your inquiry, including solar consultations, energy audits, scheduling, reminders, and follow-up communication.

Message frequency varies. Message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP.

Consent to receive SMS messages is not a condition of purchase. We do not send unsolicited messages, cold outreach, or messages using purchased, rented, or shared contact lists.

Opt-In Record Keeping

SMS consent is collected prior to any messages being sent and may be logged and stored to demonstrate compliance with applicable laws and carrier requirements.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information for marketing or promotional purposes.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. SMS opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Information may only be shared if required by law or to provide services you have explicitly requested.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or misuse.

Your Choices

  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP
  • Request access to or correction of your personal information

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
